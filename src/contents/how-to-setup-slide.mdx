# Setup Slides In Kiosk

To setup slides:

1. Go to **Settings** (Click 5 times on the footer on the start screen).
2. Choose the **Settings** section.
3. Click on the **Theme** tab.
4. Click on **Slides**.
5. Choose the image you want to change.

<Blockquote type="info">
You should choose images with the recommended resolution.
</Blockquote>
