# Setup slides in kiosk

To setup slides:

1. Go to **Setting** (Click 5 times on the footer in the start screen)
2. Choose **Settings** section
3. Hit **Theme** tab
4. Hit **Slides**
5. Choose your image to change

<Blockquote type="info">
You should choose images with the recommended resolution
</Blockquote>
