# Setup Tip Suggestion

### To Show Tip Suggestion

1. Go to the license web.
2. Choose the **Store** tab.
3. Choose your store.
4. Click on the **Credit Card** tab, then choose the **Kiosk** section.
5. In the **Tip** section, check the **Enable Tip Suggestion** checkbox.
6. Click the **Save** button.


### To Hide the "No Tip" Button

1. In the **Tip** section, check the **Hide button No Tip On Tip screen** checkbox.
2. Click the **Save** button.

### To Change Tip Suggestion Mode

1. In the **Tip** section, choose the desired **Tip Suggestion Mode** (Amount/Percent).
2. Click the **Save** button.
