# Setup Theme In Kiosk

To change the theme:

1. Go to **Settings** (Click 5 times on the footer on the start screen).
2. Choose the **Settings** section.
3. Click on the **Theme** tab.
4. Choose a theme in the **Theme** section.
5. Click the **Save** button.
