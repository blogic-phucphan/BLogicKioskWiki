# Setup settle batch automatic

To settle batch automatic

1. Open pos dashboard
2. Hit **Setting** tab
3. Choose **POS Workstation**
4. Find & choose your kiosk workstation
5. Check **Auto Settlement Credit Card Batch at** and setup time
6. Hit **Save & Exit** button
