# Change Auto Settle Time

To change the auto settle time, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Settings** (Click 5 times on the footer in the start screen).
2. Choose the **SETTINGS** button.
3. Click on the **Credit Card Settlement** section in the Devices tab.
4. Enable or disable auto settlement for the credit card batch.
5. Change the auto settle time.
6. Click **Save**.
