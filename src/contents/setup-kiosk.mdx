## Setup Your Kiosk Device


## Setup The Payment Terminal

Before setting up the payment terminal in the kiosk, we need to add the terminal to the license.

1. Choose your store
2. Click on the **CC Terminals** tab
3. Enter the terminal information 
4. Click on **Save**

<Blockquote type="warning">
Enter the correct serial number (S/N) information of the terminal device.
</Blockquote>

2. Click on **Settings** button
3. Sign in
4. Click on **Payment Terminals** in the **Devices** tab

## Setup The Printer

1. Go to **Setting** (Click 5 times on the footer on the start screen)
2. Click on **Settings** button
3. Sign in
4. Click on **Printer** in the **Devices** tab
