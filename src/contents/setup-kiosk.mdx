# Setup your kiosk device


## Setup the payment terminal

Before setup payment terminal in kiosk, We have to add terminal in license.

1. Choose your store
2. Hit **CC Terminals** tab
3. Add terminal information 
4. Hit **Save**

<Blockquote type="warning">
Enter the correct S/N information of the terminal device
</Blockquote>

<img src="/assets/setup-kiosk/8e8e2a4c7008232f248d09f1cef2b339.gif" alt="" />


1. Go to **Setting** (Click 5 times on the footer in the start screen)
2. Choose **Settings** section
3. Sign in
4. Hit **Payment Terminals** in the **Devices** tab

## Setup the printer

1. Go to **Setting** (Click 5 times on the footer in the start screen)
2. Choose **Settings** section
3. Sign in
4. Hit **Printer** in the **Devices** tab
