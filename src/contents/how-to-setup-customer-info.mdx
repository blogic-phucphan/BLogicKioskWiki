# Setup Customer Information

To set up customer information, follow these steps:

1. Go to **Settings** (Click 5 times on the footer in the start screen).
2. Choose the **SETTINGS** button.
3. Click on the **Customer Info** tab.
4. Enable or disable the customer information mandate to show/hide the form for entering customer information.
5. Set the required fields for customer name, customer phone, and customer email as desired.
6. Click **Save**.
