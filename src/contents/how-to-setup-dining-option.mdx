# Setup Dining Option

To setup the dining option:

1. Open the POS dashboard.
2. Click on the **Settings** tab.
3. Choose **Configurations**.
4. Click on the **Kiosk** tab.
5. Check the **Prompt dining option before ordering** checkbox.
6. Click the **Save** button.
