# Show Sold Out Items

To show sold out items:

1. Open the POS dashboard.
2. Click on the **Settings** tab.
3. Choose **Configurations**.
4. Click on the **Kiosk** tab.
5. Check the **Allow Special Instruction for ticket** (ticket) and **Allow Special Instruction for item** (item) options.
6. Click the **Save** button.
