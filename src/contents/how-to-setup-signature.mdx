# Setup Signature

To setup a signature:

1. Go to the license web.
2. Choose the **Store** tab.
3. Select your store.
4. Click on the **Credit Card** tab and choose the **Kiosk** section.
5. In the **Configurations** section, check the **Signature Required** checkbox and enter a value in the **Signature Required Limit** field.
6. Click the **Save** button.

<Blockquote type="warning">
   The Signature Required Limit field represents the minimum limit for a mandatory signature (Total Price >= Signature Required Limit field).
</Blockquote>
