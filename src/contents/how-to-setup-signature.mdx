# Setup signature

To setup signature

1. Go to license web 
2. Choose **Store** tab
3. Choose your store
4. Hit **Credit Card** tab, choose **Kiosk** section
5. In **Configurations** section, check **Signature Required** & enter **Signature Required Limit** field
6. Hit **Save** button

<Blockquote type="warning">
   The signature Required Limit field is minimum limit for mandatory signature (Total Price >= Signature Required Limit field)
</Blockquote>
